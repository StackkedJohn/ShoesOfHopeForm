Register a child for shoes

This form takes about 5–7 minutes. Please complete one registration per child. You'll pick your event location (Gaston or Rutherford) and then choose a shoe size and underwear size based on the child's gender. We'll email a confirmation when your registration is received.

  • We serve children in foster/kinship care, ages 2–18 (still in school in Fall 2026).
  • The child is required to attend the event, as they pick out their own things.
  • Submitting this form does not guarantee a spot — everyone receives a phone call to process their registration.

Required fields are marked with *.
